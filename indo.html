What’s the age criteria for this Program?

There are 2 levels of BrainLink Launchpad:

a. Launchpad Pre teen's : For kids from birth (0 years) until 2.5 years old, at the time of enrolment.

b. Launchpad Pre-K : For kids from 2.5 years until 6 years of age, at the time of enrolment.

What’s the process and eligibility criteria to enrol?

We invite everyone to participate in our Programs as long as they are the right fit. The process is simple: simply fill out the application form, schedule a call with our Program Manager who will assess your fit with the program, complete the payment formalities, and start with the next cohort.

How long does it take to fill the application form?

Between 1-3 minutes. It 's pretty quick.

My child already goes to a pre-school/daycare. Do I need this program? Also, will it confuse my child at school if I do your programs?

Our program is independent of whether a child goes to pre-school/daycare or not. Pre-schools are designed for 1 teacher to teach many kids for long durations. This is not the most efficient way of teaching babies, Pre teen'ss, and young kids. 

Our Programs are designed for 1-on-1 teaching and hardly take few minutes a day. They don’t interfere with anything that the child maybe doing outside of our programs. If anything, it will accelerate their progress in all aspects.

Is the content of this program different from your other Programs?

The content and Physical Materials of this Program is carefully curated and may overlap with our other Programs to provide a comprehensive, integrated journey to parents, along with a lot of other benefits as well.

I’ve already done some of your other programs. Can I still do this?

Yes, you will still be able to do this, but please fill out the application form and we might give you a customized solution with custom pricing.

When does the Program start?

If this page is active, it means that we are accepting applications for a new upcoming cohort that starts within the next 30 days.

How long is this Program for?

The Program is flexible and Parents can take their own time to finish the program at the pace that’s convenient for them. We expect parents to be able to finish each level of the Program in about 12-18 months. However, we provide Program benefits for up to 2 years so that Parents can comfortably do the Program.

Can I pause the program after I enrol?

You can delay your start date after enrolling for as long as you want. Eg., you could register today, and decide to start after a few months. However, once you have started, you can’t “pause” the program – although you will have sufficient time and flexibility to complete the Program at your own pace.

Will my child get a graduation certificate?

Yes, your child will get a certificate upon completion of the Program.

Will you help with school admissions?

While we cannot help you directly with school admissions, but your child will learn several demonstrable skills much in advance of their age. A lot of schools that are super selective, value early abilities in kids and might consider your application favourably. However, we cannot guarantee this in any way.
